FAQ

Common questions.

Is this actual legal advice? +

Term Stack provides legal information and document drafting powered by AI. For jurisdiction-specific legal advice, our Scale plan includes attorney review. We always recommend having critical documents reviewed by licensed counsel.

What are NVCA agreements? +

NVCA (National Venture Capital Association) documents are the industry-standard templates for venture financing. They include term sheets, stock purchase agreements, investor rights agreements, and more. Our Transactions Agent generates these customized to your deal terms.

How does Slack integration work? +

Install the Term Stack app in your Slack workspace. Each agent gets its own bot you can tag in any channel or DM. Ask questions, request documents, or upload contracts for review — all within your normal workflow.

Is my data secure? +

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never train on your data.

What stage startups do you support? +

Term Stack is built for pre-seed through Series B companies. Our agents cover everything from incorporation and your first SAFE to full NVCA financing packages for priced rounds.
