Where AI fits

We begin where the work is repeatable, high in volume, and open to review.

AI earns its place in specific, bounded workflows, not everywhere at once. A few of the places it tends to pay off.

i

Document review and diligence

First-pass review and issue spotting across large document sets, with a person in the loop.

ii

Contract drafting and review

Drafting from precedent, redline triage, and clause-level checks against firm standards.

iii

Research and memoranda

Grounded research and first drafts that cite back to source, kept inside firm-controlled material.

iv

Intake and matter triage

Routing, conflict surfacing, and structured capture at the front door of a matter.

v

Knowledge management

Making a firm's own work product findable and usable instead of trapped in old matters.

vi

Client-facing tools

Applications that let clients self-serve well-bounded tasks, on rails the firm defines.
